Class Sources

Classes and subclasses from the following sourcebooks are legal for play: PHB, XGE, TCE, DMG, EGW*, FTD. Classes and subclasses listed as playable on this website are legal, as are Matthew Colvilles Illrigger and Michael Wolf's Shaman.

*While both wizard subclasses from EGW are legal, Echo Knight is banned from play due to the excessive packets and questions introduced by simply choosing the class.
