Goliaths inhabit a cold, desolate region known as No Manâ€™s Land. Here, in a forsaken tundra, only the strongest survive against the bitter cold. Their tribal lives are fraught with danger, which they meet head-on as creations of the earth goddess Gravita.

Playable Traits

Ability Score Increase. Your Strength increases by 2, and your Constitution increases by 1.

Age. Goliaths have similar lifespans to humans. They enter adulthood in their late teens and usually live less than a century.

Size. Goliaths are between 7 and 8 feet tall and weigh between 280 and 340 pounds. Your size is Medium.

Speed. Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and Giant.

Natural Athlete. You have proficiency in the Athletics skill.

Menacing. You have proficiency in the Intimidation skill.

Stone's Endurance. You can focus yourself to occasionally shrug off injury. When you take damage, you can use your reaction to roll a d12. Add your Constitution modifier to the number rolled and reduce the damage by that total. Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.

Powerful Build. You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.

Mountain Born. You have resistance to cold damage. You're also acclimated to high altitude, including elevations above 20,000 feet.
