Races

Many creatures claim the world of Fehr'gia as their home and originate from a few distinct roots.

Inheritors of Fehr'gia These ancient races stem from a divergent line of evolution. Through a stroke of fate, they inherited the land of Fehr'gia and founded civilizations that rule over the mainland to this day.

  • Half Elves
  • Elves
  • Humans
  • Dwarves
  • Gnomes
  • Halflings

The Elderkin Beings of a higher power created these races for various reasons. They are not bound to their creators and are more or less free to explore the world.

  • Firbolgs
  • Half Orcs
  • Easifa
  • Goliaths
  • Kanin

The Veil-Touched Souls changed, wilfully or otherwise, by energies of otherworldly planes.

  • Genasi
  • Aasimar
  • Shifters
  • Tieflings

Children of The Reaches Denizens of the far lands known as The Reaches. They vary widely in shape, size, and lineage.

  • Melambai
  • Tabaxi
  • Minotaur
  • Satyrs
  • Lizardfolk
  • Loxodon
  • Ratlings
  • Tortles
  • Kenku
  • Aarakocra
  • Sharkfolk
  • Grung

Unnatural Souls Creatures that do not fit directly into the other races.

  • Ethereal
  • Kobolds
  • Treefolk
  • Goblin
  • Warforged
  • Bugbear
  • Hobgoblin
  • Myconids
  • Changelings
